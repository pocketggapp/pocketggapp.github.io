pocketgg

A beautiful, native iOS app for start.gg

pocketgg aims to provide the cleanest and fastest experience available for using start.gg on mobile. Powered by start.gg's GraphQL API and featuring a fully native iOS design, pocketgg is the go-to companion app for video game tournament competitors and spectators alike.

Discover Tournaments

Select your favourite games and find all of the upcoming tournaments that feature those games, both in-person and online. You can also search to find any tournament hosted on start.gg.

Details at a Glance

View essential information for every tournament like events, location, streams, all the way down to individual sets, games, and seed numbers.

Stay Connected with your local scene

Follow tournament organizers to see the next upcoming regional, or all of the locals that you hopefully haven't gone 0-2 in.

Native Bracket View

No more awkward web views that have trouble scrolling or zooming in & out. pocketgg features a native bracket view that allows you to view any single/double elimination or round-robin bracket with ease.

Your Personal Resume

Log in using your start.gg account, and view your profile to see all of the recent tournaments that you've entered.

And More

pocketgg includes other nice-to-haves, such as the ability to add tournaments to your calendar, and to pin your favourite tournaments for easy access.
